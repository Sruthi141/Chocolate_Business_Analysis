# 🍫 Chocolate Business Report Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
This project presents an interactive **Business Intelligence Dashboard** that analyzes chocolate product sales performance across multiple countries, salespersons, time periods, and product categories.

The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor shipping volumes, revenue trends, and product-level contributions to support data-driven business decisions.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Insights

### 🔹 Sales Performance by Salesperson
Displays the total number of chocolate boxes shipped by each salesperson.

**Key Benefits:**
- Identifies top-performing sales representatives
- Helps evaluate individual sales performance
- Supports incentive planning and target allocation

---

### 🔹 Sales Distribution by Country
Shows the total boxes shipped across different countries.

**Key Benefits:**
- Highlights high-demand international markets
- Supports regional demand forecasting
- Assists supply chain and logistics planning

---

### 🔹 Revenue Trend Over Time
Visualizes total sales revenue across months, quarters, and years.

**Key Benefits:**
- Identifies seasonal sales patterns
- Detects peak and low revenue periods
- Supports financial forecasting and budgeting

---

### 🔹 Product Contribution Analysis
A waterfall chart representing how different products contribute to overall revenue changes.

**Key Benefits:**
- Identifies best-performing and underperforming products
- Shows individual product impact on total revenue
- Helps optimize product strategy and marketing focus

---

## 🎯 Key Business Value
✔ Monitor sales performance across regions  
✔ Identify top salespersons  
✔ Track revenue growth trends  
✔ Understand product-level revenue impact  
✔ Enable strategic business planning  

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used
- **Power BI** – Dashboard creation and visualization
- **Excel / CSV** – Data source
- **Data Modeling** – Data transformation and aggregation

---

## 🚀 Business Impact
This dashboard improves operational visibility and supports faster, data-driven decision-making in sales, marketing, and supply chain management.
